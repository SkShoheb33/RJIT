Innovation & Entrepreneurship RJIT has adopted several programs to enrich the entrepreneurial eco-system and technology commercialization efforts at the Institute. RJIT manages the incubator program at RVR & JC.
IP & Technology Transfer RJIT manages the Intellectual Property and also facilitates the commercialization of IP assets and Know-how of RVR & JC College of Engineering through technology transfer and licensing.
Foundation for Innovation and Technology Transfer RJIT is an industry Academia interface organization, established by RVR&JC to facilitate Research Translation, Technology Development, IPR Management, Technology Transfer & Commercialisation.
ABOUT RJIT

RVR & JC Innovation Technology (RJIT), is an umbrella organisation at RVR & JC for fostering entrepreneurship and nurturing tech start-ups. It administers a buRJITss incubator which provides ‘Start to scale’ support for technology based entrepreneurship and facilitates the conversion of research activity into entrepreneurial ventures.
